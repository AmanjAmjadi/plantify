Common Plant Health Issues

Leaf Yellowing

Often caused by overwatering, nutrient deficiencies, or insufficient light. Check soil moisture and ensure proper drainage.

Brown Leaf Tips

Usually indicates low humidity or over-fertilization. Increase humidity and flush the soil to remove excess salts.

Powdery Mildew

White powdery coating on leaves caused by fungal infection. Improve air circulation and avoid overhead watering.

Spider Mites

Tiny pests causing stippled, discolored leaves. Increase humidity, spray with water, or use insecticidal soap.

Spring: Increase watering as growth accelerates. Good time for repotting.

Summer: Protect from intense afternoon sun. Water more frequently.

Fall: Reduce watering as growth slows. Stop fertilizing.

Winter: Minimal watering. Keep away from cold drafts and heaters.
